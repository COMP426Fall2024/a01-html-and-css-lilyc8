About Appa

sourced from the official Avatar fandom page

Appa is Avatar Aang's sky bison companion and served as his spirit guide. He is part of the Air Nomad civilization and was born in the Eastern Air Temple. Appa has brown eyes and has a white fur coat with brown stripes. He is able to fly and use air bending. As the Avatar's animal guide, he assists the Gaang through their journey and offers them his protection along with his loyalty. During the Hundred Year War, Appa was the last of his kind. He and Aang were the sole survivors of the East Temple Air Nomads. Because of his ability to fly, he was the main method of transportation for Team Avatar and he was an indispensable part of the team. His diet consists mostly of plants and fruits, his favorite food is hay.

Appa has a great sense of humor along with a gentle and protective nature. He quickly became friends with all of Team Avatar, with the exception of Zuko. Although he is quite large, Appa is extremely affectionate and provides comfort to the group and acts as a soft mattress for them to sleep on. Despite Appa's gentle nature, he can also be incredibly fierce and determined when the lives of his friends are in danger. He is courageous and strong in battle.
